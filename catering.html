The Process

How catering
works with us.

01

Inquiry

Tell us about your event. Size, venue, date, and what you are envisioning. No commitment, just a conversation.

02

Consultation

We review your event details and schedule a call to understand your needs, preferences, and any special requirements.

03

Custom Proposal

We send a detailed proposal with services, custom menu, staffing plan, timeline, and pricing. Tailored to your event, not templated.

We arrive early, set up completely, and run the full operation. You enjoy your event. We handle everything else.
