How far in advance should I book?

We recommend reaching out at least 2 to 4 weeks before your event, and earlier for large-scale or catering requests. Popular dates fill quickly, so do not wait.

Do you offer custom drink menus?

Absolutely. We can craft a menu around your event's theme, brand colors, or dietary needs, from signature lattes to custom matcha tonics.

What does the setup look like?

We handle arrival, full setup, and breakdown. Our trailer and carts are self-contained. We just need space, power access, and your guest list to plan the right quantities.
